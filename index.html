Founded in August 2023, PICSEL emerged with the dual mission of providing a platform for budding engineers and innovators across the country to share and disseminate ideas in science and technology. Conceived by eminent professors and a motivated group of students from the CSE department of KDKCE, PICSEL operates on a non-profit basis, upholding the vision of its founders to this day.
